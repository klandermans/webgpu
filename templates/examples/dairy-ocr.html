🔬 How It Works

📸 Camera Capture

Live video feed from your device camera captures the cow's ear tag and physical condition.

🧠 AI Vision Model

Moondream2 VLM (Vision Language Model) runs locally via WebGPU for OCR and visual analysis.

📝 Text Extraction

Ear tag numbers are extracted using optical character recognition powered by AI.

🏥 Health Assessment

Posture, cleanliness, and overall condition are analyzed for health scoring.

⚙️ Technical Details

  • Model: Moondream2 (1.4B parameters) - Vision Language Model
  • Runtime: WebGPU + Transformers.js (ONNX backend)
  • Tasks: Image-to-text, OCR, Visual Question Answering
  • Privacy: All processing happens locally - no data sent to servers
  • Requirements: Chrome 113+ or Edge 113+ with WebGPU support
  • Camera: Rear/environment camera preferred for ear tag capture
