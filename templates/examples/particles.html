Why GPU for Particles?

CPU simulation becomes slow with thousands of particles. GPU excels because:

  • Each particle update is independent - perfect for parallelization
  • GPU has thousands of cores vs CPU's few cores
  • Memory bandwidth is much higher on GPU
  • Can simulate 10,000+ particles at 60 FPS
